Sustainable travel for unpacking capitalist routine and its cultural ideologies

A few years ago I started traveling and it gave me a long lasting feeling of connection, with myself and with the world. I substituted the temporary distractions that in no way benefited me or my body and started to look at traveling as a way to reduce my stress and engage with the community while simultaneously gaining a new perspective and knowledge of the world around me. Solo travel as become specially attractive as I can move at my own pace and have much more control over my planning. Some of my favorite travelling themes include nature exploring, street art, leftist/feminist movement and macabre/abandonned places.
